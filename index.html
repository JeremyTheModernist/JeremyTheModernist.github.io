About The Sentimental Type —

Thanks for stopping by The Sentimental Type. TST was started as a personal desire to bring more emotional awareness to typography and the effect it's form can have on people. In many ways, typography is a collection of tiny little sculptures, whose purpose for readability and communication often disguises its (not so) subtle character. In an effort to accentuate the personality of these typefaces, they have been layered with imagery*. Imagery that, whether through form, feeling, history or a mixture of all three, creates context for the typeface, highlighting its own unique qualities.

The Types Of Personalities

Some of these typefaces are fierce and abrupt, while others are soft and quiet. Some speak with intellect and elegance while others speak with gravitas and a certain weight in their voice. Many share similar values but have slightly different intonations. Regardless of their voice, all typefaces, like people, are representations of their historical time period, their forms indicative of the values and technology of the age.

About the Author

Jeremy Barnes is a self-proclaimed type addict. To appease these addictions, TST was created as an effort to personify and accentuate the qualities of different typefaces. He hopes other designers will benefit from the insights gathered here.
